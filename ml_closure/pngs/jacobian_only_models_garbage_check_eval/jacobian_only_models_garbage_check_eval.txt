jacobian_only_models_garbage_check_eval — are the strong Jacobian-only R2s real physics or another artifact?

WHY THIS DIAGNOSTIC EXISTS (the morning incident, 2026-07-13)

On the morning of 2026-07-13 a "good" global R2 on the full-target
(Jacobian + body-force/penalty commutator) cylinder model turned out to be
HOLLOW: the field panels showed the prediction painting wake structure where
the truth was near zero, and the R2 was carried almost entirely by the
obstacle-ring band of Pi (the body-force commutator hugging the cylinder) —
geometry, not physics. That discovery triggered (a) the switch to
JACOBIAN-ONLY targets (variant gaussian_jonly: Pi_J = filter(J)_fine -
J(filtered); the body/sponge commutator removed, so the target has NO
obstacle ring for skill to hide in), and (b) the standing rule that every R2
must also be reported WAKE-RESTRICTED: only pixels farther than 1*D from the
body (SDF > 1*D), where a ring cannot contribute.

Tonight's order (Sanaa, 2026-07-13 night): the two in-flight Jacobian-only
trainings show strong val R2 (cylinder ~0.63-0.69 rising, cape ~0.90).
PROVE these are not garbage by re-running the SAME eval package + symlog
field panels that exposed the morning problem, plus the wake-restricted R2.
