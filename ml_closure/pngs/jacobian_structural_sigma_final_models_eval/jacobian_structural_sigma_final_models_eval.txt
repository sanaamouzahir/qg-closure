WHAT THIS FOLDER IS
Final-model evaluation of the two Jacobian-only + structural-sigma retrains
that landed overnight 2026-07-14 (run codenames piff_fpc_gjs = cylinder,
piff_cape_gjs = cape; best.pt = last epoch, ep99 of 100, for BOTH — the
early-NLL-freeze pathology of the previous runs is gone). These are the
production candidates: Jacobian-only gaussian targets, arm-F structural
noise prior sigma^2(x) = softplus(a) + softplus(b)*|grad omega|^2, and (on
the cylinder only) the upstream loss-mask x < x_c - 1.5D that removes the
undersized-sponge reflection band from the loss.

HEADLINES
- Cylinder wake-restricted R2 (same SDF > 1D mask as the 07-13 garbage
  check): 0.444 -> 0.922. The upstream repair + retrain flipped the
  cylinder verdict from "real-but-flattered" to genuinely wake-real.
- Cape wake-restricted R2: 0.954 -> 0.972; global 0.906 -> 0.935.
- Arm-F structural prior: sigma now TRACKS flow sharpness (Spearman
  sigma-vs-|grad omega| 0.75 cylinder / 0.84-0.92 cape; it was dead-flat,
  ratio 1.0002x, on the full-target reference). BUT the response is
  floor-dominated: sigma sits at the softplus(a) floor for ~90% of pixels
  and only the top gradient decile engages the b-term, under-responding
  ~3x where the error jumps 8.8x. Coverage@1sigma is still ~0.99 both
  models: the remaining overdispersion is the FREESTREAM FLOOR
  (cov1 freestream 0.999 vs near-body 0.84) — a per-term (a,b)
  recalibration is the identified next lever, not more training.
