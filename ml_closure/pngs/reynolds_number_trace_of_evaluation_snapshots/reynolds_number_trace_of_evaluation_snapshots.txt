REYNOLDS-NUMBER TRACE OF THE EVALUATION SNAPSHOTS
=================================================
THE QUESTION THIS FOLDER ANSWERS: which flow regimes do the six field
panels of each evaluation actually sample?

EVERY PNG SHOWS: the inlet Reynolds number of each evaluated member over
simulation time (read from the simulation's inlet-speed control table),
with black dashed vertical lines at the times of the six field snapshots
shown in pngs/field_panels_truth_vs_prediction/.
INPUTS: the member's stored inlet-speed table; no model involved.
COMPARES: snapshot times vs the Re trajectory -- confirms the six panels
were spread across the full Re range, not cherry-picked from one regime.
PURPOSE: context figure. When a field panel looks bad, check here FIRST
what regime it was in (e.g. the top of a ramp vs a quiet stretch).

FILES: <model>_inlet_reynolds_trace_with_snapshot_times.png for the
production model, cape baseline, grid winner, and the two evaluated smoke
tests. The traces for the cross-member tests live with them in
pngs/cross_member_generalization_of_single_member_model/.
