WHAT THIS FILE IS: the 2026-07-13 triage decomposition of the predicted
uncertainty for the production cylinder model -- how much of sigma^2 comes from the learned
constant NOISE term vs from the Gaussian-process POSTERIOR variance (the
part that can actually vary across the flow).
PURPOSE: if the flat noise term dominates, sigma is structurally unable to
track the flow (the per-pixel shape miscalibration seen in
pngs/model_uncertainty_vs_flow_sharpness/); the companion figure is in
pngs/uncertainty_decomposition_noise_vs_gp_variance/.
ARRAYS INSIDE:
  members             the simulation members evaluated
  noise_phys          the learned constant noise variance, physical units
  posterior_var_mean  mean GP posterior variance per member
  ratio               noise / posterior-variance ratio per member (large =
                      sigma is mostly a flat floor)
  decile_<member>     per-member decile profiles of the components
